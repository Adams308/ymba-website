About YMBA

YMBA (Youth Microfinance & Business Academy) was founded to make entrepreneurship accessible to teens who lack capital or formal training. We combine short training workshops with micro-grants and mentorship—building skills and confidence that last far beyond the program.

Our approach

  • Practical workshops — real skills, no jargon
  • Seed micro-grants with simple repayment plans
  • Peer mentorship and community accountability

Founder

Emmanuel Akoto — community organizer and youth empowerment advocate. Raised in Abokobi-Sesemi, Emmanuel built YMBA after leading local projects in financial literacy and youth entrepreneurship.

Contact Emmanuel

How it works

  1. Apply: Short application & interview
  2. Train: Three weekly workshops
  3. Launch: Receive a micro-grant
  4. Grow: Weekly mentoring & reporting

Partners

Local schools, community centers, and volunteer mentors. We welcome more partners who care about youth-led enterprise.

Programs

Micro-Grant Labs

Quick-cycle funding for high-impact, low-cost ventures—paired with coaching and simple repayment systems.

Business Essentials

Pricing, budgeting, inventory, customer care, and profit-tracking taught via role-play and shop simulations.

Market Days & Demo Sales

Opportunities to test products, get customer feedback, and access local markets through pop-up events.
